Privacy Policy

[Effective Date: TBD]

Luna is a women's health companion built and operated by OTR MEDIA LLC. We know that the information you share with Luna is among the most personal data a person can hold. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the rights you have over it. We have written it in plain language because your privacy should never be hidden behind legalese.

This policy applies to the Luna mobile application and to iamluna.app. It is designed to meet the requirements of the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as well as other applicable privacy laws.

Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal information is:

OTR MEDIA LLC
30 N Gould St Ste N
Sheridan, WY 82801-6317
United States

For any privacy question or request, contact us at contact@iamluna.app.

Information We Collect

We collect only what Luna needs to work well for you.

  • Account information: your email address and the name you choose to provide when you create an account.
  • Cycle and health data: the information you log, such as period dates, symptoms, moods, fertility signals, pregnancy details, and postpartum notes.
  • In-app usage: basic, privacy-respecting information about how you use Luna, used to keep the app stable and to improve it.
  • AI companion conversations: the messages you exchange with Luna's AI companion, so it can respond helpfully and remember context within your account.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for a limited set of purposes:

  • To provide the service: running your account, storing your logs, and powering the features you use.
  • To personalize predictions: using your cycle and health data to estimate phases, ovulation windows, and upcoming dates.
  • To improve the product: understanding which features help, fixing problems, and making Luna more reliable and useful.

Special Category Health Data

Much of the information you share with Luna is considered special category data under Article 9 of the GDPR. We process this data only on the basis of your explicit consent, which you provide when you choose to log health information in the app. You can withdraw that consent at any time by deleting the relevant data or your account.

We follow the principle of data minimization: we process the minimum information necessary to deliver the features you ask for, and nothing more.

What We Never Do

Some commitments are absolute.

  • We never sell your data.
  • We never use your data to train AI models.
  • We never share your data with advertisers.

Your cycle, your symptoms, and your conversations with Luna belong to you.

Third-Party Services

To run Luna, we rely on a small number of trusted service providers. Each processes data only as needed to deliver their service.

  • Supabase: securely stores your account and health data. Data is hosted in an EU region.
  • Anthropic: processes messages sent to Luna's AI companion to generate responses. Anthropic does not use this content to train its models.
  • Apple and Google: handle in-app purchases and subscriptions through their respective app stores. We do not receive or store your full payment details.

Your Rights

If you are in the European Union, the GDPR gives you the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erase your data ("the right to be forgotten").
  • Port your data to another service in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restrict certain processing of your data.
  • Object to processing in certain circumstances.

If you are a California resident, the CCPA gives you equivalent rights, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights. Because we do not sell personal information, there is no sale for you to opt out of.

To exercise any right, email contact@iamluna.app. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

Data Retention

While your account is active, we retain your data so Luna can keep working for you. If you cancel your account, we retain your data for six months and then permanently delete it, unless a longer period is required by law. You can request immediate deletion at any time.

Children's Privacy

Luna is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

International Transfers

OTR MEDIA LLC is based in the United States. If you use Luna from the European Union or another region, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We take steps to ensure your data remains protected in line with this policy and applicable law.

Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and encryption at rest. Our database uses row-level security (RLS), so each user can access only their own records. No system is perfectly secure, but we work continuously to keep your information safe.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you in the app before they take effect. The latest version will always be available here.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Reach us anytime at contact@iamluna.app.
