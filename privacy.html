Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Luna ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Luna, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data We Collect

To provide personalized cycle, fertility, and pregnancy insights, Luna collects only the data you choose to share with us:

  • Email address (for sign-in)
  • Cycle data: period dates, cycle length, symptoms, mood entries
  • Profile context: birth date, mode (cycle / pregnancy / postpartum), goals
  • Pregnancy data (if applicable): due date, milestones
  • Postpartum data (if applicable): baby's birth date, breastfeeding status
  • Journal entries you write in the app
  • Conversations with the Luna AI companion
  • Notification preferences

We do not collect contacts, location, photos, or any data unrelated to your wellbeing journey.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Calculate cycle phase, fertile window, and predictions
  • Personalize insights, articles, and AI companion responses
  • Send notifications you've opted into

Your data is never sold, never used for advertising, and never shared with marketers.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL hosted on AWS, encrypted in transit and at rest).

When you chat with the Luna AI companion, your message and a brief snapshot of your cycle context are sent to Anthropic to generate a response. Anthropic does not use this data to train models and discards it after processing.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate Luna:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and hosting
  • Anthropic — AI model for the Luna companion
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — distribution and subscription payments
  • Apple — Sign-In with Apple authentication
  • Google — Sign-In with Google authentication

No personally identifiable information is shared with advertisers or third parties for marketing.

5. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • View and edit your profile data inside the app
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Delete your account, which removes all your data within 90 days
  • Disable notifications or AI features

To request data access or deletion, email us at contact@iamluna.app.

6. Health & Medical Disclaimer

Luna is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. Information provided by the app, including AI-generated responses, is for educational purposes only and is not a substitute for advice from a qualified healthcare professional.

7. Children's Privacy

Luna is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, reach out to us at:

contact@iamluna.app
